Festivals

Caracoles Festival:
A celebration of the island's marine resources that takes place on the last Saturday of April.

Kabasan Festival:
The "Kabasan Festival" in Samal, Philippines, means "festival of seashells" in the native Sama dialect, referring to the abundance of seashells found on the island and celebrating the local Sama culture through beach activities, bikini contests, and maritime heritage events.

Hugyaw Madayaw Festival:
"Hugyaw" is a Cebuano word meaning "to roar in excitement". "Madayaw" is a Dabawenyo word meaning "good, valuable, superior, or beautiful".

Clean-ups

Zero Waste Month Clean-up: A clean-up activity that takes place in January.

Environmental Activities

Enviro-Fiesta for Kids: An environmental learning day for elementary school students that takes place in March.
