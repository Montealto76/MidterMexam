Beaches

Kaputian Beach:
A white-sand beach in the Kaputian District of Samal Island in Davao City, Philippines. The area is known for its clear waters and stunning landscapes.

Alorro Beach:
A popular beach on Samal Island in the Philippines.

Pearl Farm Beach Resort:
A luxurious resort with a Bluebird View Deck that overlooks Malipano Island.

Nature Spots

Giant Clam Sanctuary:
A marine sanctuary where visitors can snorkel and learn about giant clams.

Coral Garden Marine Park:
A protected marine sanctuary and dive site located in Talikud Island near Samal.

Monfort Bat Sanctuary:
A wildlife refuge and eco-park home to a large colony of fruit bats. A popular tourist destination.

Hagimit Falls:
A scenic waterfall on Samal Island with multiple levels and pools of running water.
